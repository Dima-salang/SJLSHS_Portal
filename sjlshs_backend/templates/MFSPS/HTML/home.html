Academic Offerings

Discover the tracks and strands offered by the school

STEM

The STEM strand is focused on the study of science and its related subjects, like math, technology, and engineering. It also includes coursework that can help prepare you for college-level science classes, like chemistry, physics, and biology. You'll learn how to apply critical thinking, problem-solving, and communication skills in the classroom and workplace.

Learn More

ABM

Students in an accountancy and business management (ABM) strand can learn a wide range of skills and knowledge that are relevant to careers in finance, business administration, and related fields. They'll develop critical thinking, problem-solving, communication, and teamwork skills, which can be useful in a variety of career paths.

Learn More

HUMSS

The humanities are a broad field of study that includes a range of disciplines that explore the human experience, including history, literature, philosophy, art, music, and culture. In the HUMSS strand, students will learn about the history and development of different cultures and societies, and the ways in which these cultures have influenced and been influenced by art, literature, and other forms of human expression.

Learn More

TVL

The technical-vocational-livelihood (TVL) strand provides students with hands-on training and practical skills in a variety of technical and vocational fields, including culinary arts, automotive technology, computer hardware servicing, and others. Students in this strand will learn how to apply their knowledge and skills in real-world settings, preparing them for careers in the fields they choose to specialize in. The TVL strand also emphasizes the importance of entrepreneurship and the development of business skills.

Learn More

Welcome to San Jose Litex Senior High School

At SJLSHS, we believe that education should be a journey of discovery and growth. Our community of students, teachers, and parents work together to provide our students with the tools they need to succeed both in and beyond the classroom. We offer a range of programs and resources designed to support our students' academic and personal growth, including:

  • Highly qualified teachers who are dedicated to their students' success
  • A challenging and engaging curriculum that prepares students for college and career
  • State-of-the-art facilities and resources that promote learning and exploration
  • A supportive and inclusive community that values diversity and promotes mutual respect

We believe that every student has the potential to achieve great things, and we are committed to helping them reach their goals. Whether you are a current student, a prospective student, or a member of our community, we invite you to explore all that SJLSHS has to offer.
