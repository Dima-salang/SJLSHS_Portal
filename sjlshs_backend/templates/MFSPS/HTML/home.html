Academic Offerings

Discover the tracks and strands offered by the school

STEM

The STEM strand is a great place to start when you're looking for a career in science. The STEM strand is focused on the study of science and its related subjects, like math, technology and engineering. It also includes coursework that can help prepare you for college-level science classes, like chemistry, physics and biology. The STEM strand gives you an opportunity to develop your critical thinking, problem-solving and communication skills. You'll learn how to apply those skills in the classroom and workplace. You'll have opportunities to work with professionals in the field of science so you can gain insights into how they do their jobs.

ABM

Students in an accountancy and business management (ABM) strand can learn a wide range of skills and knowledge that are relevant to careers in finance, business administration, and related fields. Also, students in an ABM strand may also develop skills in areas such as critical thinking, problem-solving, communication, and teamwork. These skills can be useful in a variety of career paths, including in finance, business administration, and related fields.

HUMSS

The humanities are a broad field of study that includes a range of disciplines that explore the human experience, including history, literature, philosophy, art, music, and culture In the HUMSS strand, students will learn about the history and development of different cultures and societies, and the ways in which these cultures have influenced and been influenced by art, literature, and other forms of human expression. They might will study the ideas and theories of philosophers, and explore the ways in which these ideas have shaped and continue to shape our understanding of the world.

TVL

The Technical-Vocational Livelihood strand is designed to provide students with the technical skills and knowledge needed for careers in various technical and vocational fields, as well as to provide students with the necessary foundation for entrepreneurship. TVL offers different specialized tracks such as Information and Communications Technology (ICT), Shielded Metal Arc Welding (SMAW), Home Economics, and Automotive. Students in the TVL strand may also have the opportunity to learn about entrepreneurship, which involves starting and running a business. This can include topics such as business planning, marketing, and financial management.
